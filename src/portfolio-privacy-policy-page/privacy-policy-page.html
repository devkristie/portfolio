Introduction

Welcome to my portfolio website privacy policy page. This policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit kristielarke.infy.uk. By accessing this website, you agree to the terms outlined below. This policy applies only to this website and not to any third-party sites or services linked from it.

Use Of hCaptcha

I use the hCaptcha security service (provided by Intuition Machines, Inc., Delaware, US) to ensure that interactions with my website are performed by humans and not automated bots. hCaptcha may collect information such as IP address, time spent on the site, or mouse movements. This information is forwarded to hCaptcha and processed as outlined in their Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Legal basis under GDPR:

Art. 6(1)(b) - necessary for performing a contract (website usage)
Art. 6(1)(f) - legitimate interest in preventing abuse or spam
IMI acts as a data processor under GDPR and as a service provider under CCPA.

Contact Form Data

The contact form on kristielarke.infy.uk collects the information you provide (name, email address, phone number, and message). This information is sent securely to the website owner solely to respond to inquiries. It will not be shared with third parties except as required by law. Data is retained only as long as necessary to address your request and is stored securely. I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties.

Cookies

This website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies may store preferences such as dark mode settings and help secure form submissions. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used, and only first-party cookies are employed. By continuing to use this website, you acknowledge the use of these cookies.

Data Security

I take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Some demo applications use shared, unauthenticated databases for demonstration purposes. Users are advised not to enter real personal information into these applications.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that I hold. To exercise these rights, please contact me through the website's contact form.

Privacy Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in practices or applicable laws. Significant changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Information
